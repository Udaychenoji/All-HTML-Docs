About

Founded in 1990 by a group of young scholars, HRD Degree and PG College has carved a niche for itself in the educational map of Osmania University. True to its name, the very aim of its promoters is to explore the talents of its students by expanding the horizons of education, keeping abreast of trends in vogue. The college is justly proud of its steady growth in the last 18 years, no mean achievement in the midst of competition in the private sector.
